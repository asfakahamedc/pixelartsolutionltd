# Security Policy

Pixelart Solution Ltd maintains strict security standards for its digital infrastructure and client deployments.

## Supported Architecture

We actively monitor and issue security updates exclusively for our current production stack:

| Architecture | Status |
|---|---|
| Next.js 14 (App Router) Edge Deployments | :white_check_mark: Supported |
| Legacy WordPress/PHP Deployments | :x: Unsupported / Deprecated |

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a potential vulnerability within the pixelartsolution.agency domain or its underlying headless infrastructure, do not disclose it publicly.

Report the issue directly via email to the Founder at `antigravity@pixelart.com`.

### Response SLA:

- **Acknowledgment of report:** Within 12 hours.
- **Critical infrastructure patching:** Within 24 hours.

We prioritize the security and anonymity of our corporate clients. All valid reports will be handled with strict confidentiality.
